This page features Pulsar's custom YouTube video player. It directly interfaces with and leverages the entire YouTube <iframe> API to render YouTube embeds directly into the view. AngularJS methods are used to control the video and update the view's model in real time as the videos are playing.

When creating a post with YouTube embeds, it is literally impossible for them to not be the right size, play when asked, etc., unless you're having a problem beyond this application (network connection failed). And, that's because videos go into the Videos content channel of Pulsar. A "video" is a post. There is no difference. You just choose the location of the video embed, provide Pulsar the videoId value from YouTube (or paste a link) and Pulsar directly and correctly embeds the video using whatever latest/greatest technology Google gives the world to do this.
