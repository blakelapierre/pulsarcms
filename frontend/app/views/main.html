Pulsar Helps People Create and Communicate

Pulsar is deceptively simple by design, and it's getting simpler by the day with every iteration as the project continues to evolve from a prototype/collection of experiments to a formally defined application. It's an easy yet very technologically advanced way to manage your stuff online, keep in touch with and share with those who matter most to you and even develop an application that does whatever you need it to do.

Starting with Pulsar Composer, Pulsar gives you an inplace editor that lets you see exactly what your pulses are going to look like when you publish them. There is absolutely no difference between what a Pulse will look like in Composer and what it will look like as a published Pulse. They are simply the same thing.

Pulsar makes use of CKEditor, an advanced HTML5 editor written in JavaScript. CKEditor has a plugin API that Pulsar uses to provide custom elements and other features. CKEditor together with Pulsar's extensions to it are what are known as Pulsar Composer. The goal is to help you achieve better results, better-looking posts and a more usable site by helping you focus on your message more than your HTML and CSS.
