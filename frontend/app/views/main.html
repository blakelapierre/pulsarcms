What Is Pulsar?

Pulsar is similar to blogging software. Instead of posts, Pulsar creates pulses. Pulses range from static documents (similar to standard blog posts) to highly interactive, dynamic video games and everything in between. Watch Moving Parts. Then, have a look at Prototype. from any other blogging and content management systems. It is so open and modular that you can build your own applications against the Pulsar API server, and have every feature of Pulsar integrated into whatever you're building.

From personal blogs to news sites to music sites to online games to enterprise applications of all types, Pulsar wants to be a technology you can use to quickly meet your content and website management goals. It is easy to deploy, easy to maintain, handles remarkable levels of traffic and scale and sits atop some of the best modern technology.
