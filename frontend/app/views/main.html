Welcome

Pulsar is a cross between a Content Management System and an application development platform. It helps people create. The audio player above is not a SoundCloud embedded player. That is the Pulsar presentation engine building one for you. It can look and feel however you want.

The easiest way to get involved and help push Pulsar forward is to create a profile, have a look around and post new content here to see how Pulsar works.

Test Drive: SoundCloud!

This widget currently requires Google Chrome.

The following is an example of Pulsar's built-in SoundCloud player. All you need to embed one or more SoundCloud sound(s) into your pulses is the URL of the sound that you would share on social media. Using Pulsar Composer's in-place editor, you just put the cursor where you want the sound and click the SoundCloud button in the tool bar. Paste the URL and you're done.
