How Is Pulsar Different or Better?

Watch Moving Parts. Then, have a look at Prototype. Those links should say everything that's needed for now to help answer how Pulsar is different from any other blogging and content management systems. It is so open and modular that you can build your own applications against the Pulsar API server, and have every feature of Pulsar integrated into whatever you're building.

From personal blogs to news sites to music sites to online games to enterprise applications of all types, Pulsar wants to be a technology you can use to quickly meet your content and website management goals. It is easy to deploy, easy to maintain, handles remarkable levels of traffic and scale and sits atop some of the best modern technology.
