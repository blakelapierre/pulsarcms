Welcome to Pulsar!

Pulsar is a new content management system being built with one very important primary concern: High-quality user experiences.

Pulsar is built on the MEAN stack, and my intention is to keep it simple. I am just getting started. Learn more...

Sample Blog

This is a sample of a blog built with Pulsar. The data model and API already support likes and dislikes. I'm working on the UI for those. Comments are implemented. Open any post and scroll down. No need to create an account or login to comment on a post. I have other ways of preventing spam. Just enjoy the blog and feel free to leave some feedback. This is intended to be a discsussion and not just a demonstration of what Pulsar can do.
