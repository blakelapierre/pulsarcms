About Pulsar Studio

Pulsar Studio is a timeline-driven authoring tool that runs here in the browser and enables content creators to embed a key piece of media into a Pulse. Once embedded and tagged as the key media in the Pulse, creators can then synchronize presentation events to the media's current time as it plays/scrubs/seeks.

Pulsar Studio intends to make the creation of highly interactive slide shows, entertainment and educational experiences incredibly easy and possibly even a degree of fun.
