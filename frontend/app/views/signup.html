About Your Account

Your email address is your Pulsar identity. While it isn't shown to anyone or used for anything else you don't choose, it is the ID you must provide along with your provided password to login and create content on Pulsar. An email will be sent to the address provided. You will not be permitted to create content on Pulsar until you've verified your email address.

Your display name is the name others will read when they see you online, read your posts and comments and more. Basically, that's the name you want people to read so we don't have to call you yourname@someservice.com (even if that's not your email address, it's what we'd call you without a display name). It doesn't even need to be unique. You can all be Oompa Loompa. But, if you're name-spoofing, people will probably block you. It'll be interesting to see if there can, in fact, only be one Ooompa Loompa, or if more than one will be a real problem.

Please, though...take a moment and choose a password to keep your blog and content safe. Treat it like a car buying experience. And, if you need a little emotional support or perhaps spiritual guidance during the password creation process, it's always best to keep trying until "Very Strong" is displayed where "Very Weak" used to be as you started trying. You won't be allowed to submit the form until the passwords at least match.
